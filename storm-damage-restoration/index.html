Storm & Hail Damage Restoration in San Angelo, TX

When hail and high winds open up a roof, rain follows it inside. Vetted local pros stop the water intrusion, dry the interior, and restore the damage the storm left behind.

📞 Call (000) 000-0000 Free Estimate

San Angelo sits squarely in Texas hail country. A single spring or summer supercell can drop hail large enough to bruise or puncture a roof, tear flashing loose and drive rain into the attic and walls — sometimes before you even realize the roof is compromised.

Storm-related water damage is a race against the next rain. Once the roof envelope is breached, every following shower adds more water to insulation, ceilings and wall cavities. This service focuses on the water side of storm recovery: stopping the intrusion and drying and restoring the interior.

Storm damage we help with

  • Roof-leak water intrusion — water entering through hail-punctured or wind-lifted roofing.
  • Ceiling and attic water damage — soaked insulation, sagging drywall and stained ceilings.
  • Wind-driven rain — water forced in around windows, doors and damaged siding.
  • Wet wall cavities — hidden moisture that leads to mold if not dried properly.

What the pros do first

Step one is to stop more water from coming in — often with temporary measures like tarping or board-up until permanent roof repairs can be made. From there, the process mirrors any water loss: locate all the moisture with meters and thermal imaging, extract any standing water, then dry the structure with air movers and dehumidifiers. Saturated insulation and severely damaged drywall are removed; sound materials are dried in place.

Documenting for your insurance claim

Sudden storm damage — hail and wind — is one of the most commonly covered perils on a Texas homeowners policy. That makes documentation especially important. The pros photograph the damage, log moisture readings, and note the storm date so your claim clearly ties the water damage to the weather event. Good records make the difference between a smooth claim and a fight. See our insurance guide for details.

Move quickly after the storm

After a major hail event, demand for roofers and restoration crews spikes across the Concho Valley. Calling early gets your property protected and dried before secondary damage — like mold or a collapsed ceiling — sets in. We connect you with local pros serving San Angelo, Grape Creek, Wall, Christoval and surrounding towns. For related help, see structural drying and mold remediation.
