About San Angelo Water Damage Pros

A simpler, faster way for the Concho Valley to get water damage handled by trusted local professionals.

San Angelo Water Damage Pros is a local dispatch and referral service. When you have a water emergency, we connect you with vetted, experienced water damage restoration pros serving San Angelo and the surrounding Concho Valley — quickly, any time of day or night.

What we do

We're not a national call center reading from a script, and we don't pretend to own a fleet of trucks and crews. Instead, we focus on one thing: making it fast and easy for local homeowners and businesses to reach a qualified restoration professional when water strikes. You make one call, describe what's happening, and we get you connected with a pro who can inspect, extract, dry and restore your property.

Why we started

Water damage is stressful and time-sensitive, and in the middle of an emergency the last thing you want is to sift through a dozen listings, wondering who's reputable and who will actually pick up the phone at 2 a.m. We built this service to take that burden off your shoulders — a single, dependable local point of contact backed by pros who know West Texas homes, weather and water.

How we're different

  • Always available. Our line is answered 24/7, because water emergencies don't keep business hours.
  • Truly local. The pros we work with live and work in the Concho Valley and understand our flash floods, hail storms, hard water and slab foundations.
  • Honest and straightforward. Free estimates, clear communication, and no pressure or scare tactics — just an accurate assessment of what's needed.
  • Insurance-savvy. The pros document your loss thoroughly to support your claim.

Our promise

To answer when you call, to connect you quickly with a capable local pro, and to treat your home or business — and your time — with respect. That's it. No gimmicks.
