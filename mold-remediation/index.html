Mold Remediation in San Angelo, TX

Where water sits, mold follows. Vetted local pros contain the affected area, remove the mold safely, and fix the moisture source so it doesn't come back.

📞 Call (000) 000-0000 Free Estimate

Mold needs only moisture, a food source and time — and after water damage, it has all three. In the right conditions, mold can begin to colonize damp drywall, wood and insulation in as little as 24 to 48 hours.

People sometimes assume mold isn't an issue in a semi-arid climate like San Angelo's. But mold doesn't care what the weather is doing outside — it responds to moisture inside your walls and floors. A slab leak under a bathroom, a slow drip behind a dishwasher, or an untreated water loss creates a damp, dark pocket where mold thrives regardless of how dry it is out on the plains.

How professional remediation works

  • Assessment — locating the mold and, just as importantly, the moisture feeding it.
  • Containment — sealing off the work area with plastic sheeting and negative air pressure so spores don't spread.
  • Air filtration — HEPA air scrubbers capture airborne spores during the work.
  • Removal — moldy porous materials are removed; salvageable surfaces are HEPA-vacuumed and treated.
  • Cleaning & treatment — antimicrobial application to remaining surfaces.
  • Fixing the source — drying and addressing the leak or moisture problem so mold can't return.

Why the moisture source matters most

You can scrub away every visible spot of mold, but if the underlying moisture isn't fixed, it will grow right back. That's why proper remediation always includes finding and correcting the water source — and why mold work goes hand in hand with structural drying and water damage restoration.

Signs you may have a mold problem

Call for an inspection if you notice a persistent musty odor, visible dark or fuzzy growth, discoloration on walls or ceilings, or worsening allergy-like symptoms indoors. Any of these — especially after a past leak or flood — is worth checking before it spreads.
